Beatriz Merlo

Beatriz is the only daughter of Erdutza and Carlos, and their second (middle) child, another halfling. She managed to inherit oracle abilities she's learning to hone and control. She's friends with Dante Moon.

Appearance

Body

She stands at about 5'2. Beatriz inherited her tan complexion from her mother. She has hetrochromia, so her right eye is her late father's light brown color while her left eye is her mother's dark blue. Frame-wise she's stockier than her classmates. Her hair is jet black(almost blue) and wavy like her mothers. It falls just past her shoulders.

Clothing

Her style mixes academia and cottage-core. She loves dresses, skirts and flowers, but usually patterned on collarshirts and sweaters and such.

Personality

Beatriz can become quite nozy when she's even remotely curious about something.

Background

Childhood

She's always looked up to how smart Aaron is as her big brother. Her oracle powers started manifesting at around 8 years old, a few months before Carlos died.
