Locations that appear throughout my stories.

Residentials

Merlo Home

Home of Erdutza, Aaron, Beatriz, and Eric. There is one dog too named Tomato. Carlos lived here for many years before he died.

Kraus Home

Home of Isaac, Simon and Edith. The Merlo children come here often.

Moon Family Home

Where Dante and Ji Yu live. There was an attempt at arson.

Williams family home

Where Abraham, Masego, Thomas and Amiya currently live.

Jones family home

Where the adoptive parents of Masego and Mahari live. Cookouts are often held here.

Pham-Ardelean home

Home of Daniel, Eva, Jane, Benett, Darcy and Catherine

Bishop mansion

Home of Valerian Bishop, a longtime venture capitalist who owns many buildings in town. Mirela Lupei comes to live here when her dad is in jail. After Valerian gets arested the mansion is entrusted to the Lupei family.

Human world locations

Black Bird Music

The building where Erdutza's music company resides. Their's is the biggest company there, but there's a few start ups in the building too.

Our Lady of Good Counsel Catholic Church

The Merlo children attend the schools attached to this church. Mass is attended regularly by the Merlo and Kraus. Where Jude will become the new priest. Mother Qadir helps teach here too.

11th precinct

Where Homicide detective Kraus works. Bennett interns there. Daniel Pham used to work here before he got fired.

Kennedy High School

Where Amiya and Abraham attends high school. Bennett and Darcy are students here as well.

St. Agatha Hospital

Known as one of the best hospitals in the city. Where Dr Ito, Dr Powell and Dr Zeru Mendoza work.

Lestrange academy

Danielle, Amadeus and Sue come here to try to find permanent dwelling.

St Marianne museum of history

Castelmito italy

A small town in the Calabria region in Italy. Home of Project Chimera

Inhuman locations

Alfheim

Where most sprites come from. A world parallel to Earth.

Starlight Court

One of the major islands in Alfheim. Birtplace of the Merlo and Mendoza families. Very mountainous and full of forests. Sound and wind magic are some specialties.

Moonlight Court

Birthplace of the Moon family. Specialties include illusion and potion magic.

Sunlight Court

Specialties include healing and fire magic. Many forestmakers are born here. Natives were nearly wiped out.
